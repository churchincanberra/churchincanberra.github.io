                            <p><br>
Praise the Lord&nbsp;Saints,<br>
<br>
This email gives details regarding the upcoming&nbsp; April Retreat Activities .<br>
<br>
<u>Retreat</u><br>
Saints from Sydney and Newcastle will be visiting Canberra to experience the richness of our Lord Jesus Christ with us.&nbsp;There will be messages for spiritual nourishment, sightseeing and recreational activities. This retreat is open for all university aged students, year 11 &amp; 12 students and young working Christians.<br>
<br>
Location:&nbsp; Canberra Meeting Hall<br>
Cost: $25&nbsp;for Canberra and Wagga<br>
Schedule: <a href="https://mcusercontent.com/12a6ecea8fbc1ad37a233cac1/files/46ab1fd9-c34d-6fc1-7c02-0ec0551f699a/April_2026_Retreat_Schedule.docx" target="_blank">click here</a><br>
Registration: <a href="https://pci.jotform.com/form/260753390054051" target="_blank">click here</a>&nbsp;(No&nbsp;registration needed if only attending sightseeing on Saturday and on LD)<br>
<br>
<u>BBQ Lunch &amp; Sightseeing</u><br>
All saints&nbsp;are welcome to join the BBQ Lunch and Sightseeing with the visiting young people on Saturday.<br>
<br>
Location:&nbsp; Sundown Villas BBQ area&nbsp;(<a href="https://www.google.com/maps/place/205+Narrabundah+Ln,+Symonston+ACT+2609/@-35.3472045,149.154265,524m/data=!3m2!1e3!4b1!4m6!3m5!1s0x6b164c7c84a240b9:0x17e4d702036f1699!8m2!3d-35.3470208!4d149.1551302!16s%2Fg%2F11cjgcypvt?entry=ttu&amp;g_ep=EgoyMDI2MDMyNC4wIKXMDSoASAFQAw%3D%3D" target="_blank">click here for maps</a>)&nbsp;205 Narrabundah Ln, Symonston ACT 2609<br>
Food: All provided (BBQ)<br>
Schedule: 11:30am - 3pm, Saturday the 4th of April<br>
Activity: Lunch&nbsp;will be followed by sightseeing&nbsp;of the old &amp; new Parliament house and&nbsp;War memorial&nbsp;with the visiting saints. Local saints are welcome to join.<br>
<br>
<u>Lord's Day Change of Schedule</u><br>
11am - Lord's Table Meeting&nbsp;<br>
12:30pm - Love Feast<br>
<br>
<u>Serving during the retreat&nbsp;</u><br>
Lord's Day lunch will be a love feast. We encourage the saints to bring a dish for at least&nbsp;10-15 people. Please let Brother Dermie and Sister April&nbsp;know what you will bring by&nbsp;Friday&nbsp;evening so more food can be organised if needed.<br>
<br>
<u>Working Bee&nbsp;&nbsp;</u><br>
There will be a working bee and general clean-up of the hall this Friday (3rd April) from 8am to 11am. Lunch&nbsp;will be provided<br>
<br>

In Him,<br>
<br>
The Brothers in Canberra</p>
